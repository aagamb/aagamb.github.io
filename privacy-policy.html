Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Jinendra Archana ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Favorites Data: When you add texts to your favorites, this information is stored locally on your device
  • Reading Preferences: Your reading mode settings and opacity preferences are stored locally on your device

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Basic device information necessary for app functionality

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Photos or media from your device
  • Browsing history outside the App
  • Financial information

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Improve user experience and app performance
  • Store your favorites and reading preferences locally
  • Analyze app usage to enhance features
  • Provide customer support when requested

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All user data (favorites, preferences) is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults
  • No personal data is transmitted to external servers
  • Data remains on your device unless you delete the App

Security Measures

  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access to any data is limited to authorized personnel only

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With your explicit consent

Third-Party Services

Our App may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and updates
  • iOS System Services: For basic app functionality

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • You can view and modify your favorites at any time within the App
  • You can adjust reading preferences in the App settings
  • You can delete the App to remove all locally stored data

Data Portability

  • Your favorites and preferences are stored locally and can be backed up through your device's backup system

Right to Deletion

  • Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data
  • You can contact us to request additional data deletion assistance

International Users

European Union (GDPR)

If you are in the EU, you have additional rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

California (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Say no to the sale of personal information
  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Equal service and price

Data Retention

  • Favorites and preferences are retained locally until you delete them or uninstall the App

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • For significant changes, we may provide additional notice
